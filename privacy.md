# Privacy Policy for lungilemhango.co.za

**Last Updated: 21 May 2024**

This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is collected, used, and shared when you visit or use the contact form on lungilemhango.co.za (the "Site").

## Information We Collect

When you use the contact form on our Site, we collect the following information from you:

*   Your Name
*   Your Email Address
*   Your Message

We refer to this information as "Contact Information."

## How We Use Your Information

We use the Contact Information we collect solely to respond to your inquiries, questions, or booking requests submitted through the contact form. Communication will be directly related to the content of your message.

## Sharing Your Information

Your privacy is important to us. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties.

The contact form is powered by Formspree. When you submit the form, your information is sent to Formspree's servers to be processed and forwarded to our contact email address. You can view Formspree's privacy policy for more information on how they handle data.

## Your Rights (POPIA Compliance)

In compliance with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA), you have the right to:

1.  **Access your information:** You can request a copy of the personal information we hold about you.
2.  **Correct your information:** You can request to have any inaccuracies in your personal information corrected.
3.  **Delete your information:** You can request that we delete the personal information we hold about you.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided on the Site.

## Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no internet-based site can be 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Changes

We may update this privacy policy from time to time in order to reflect, for example, changes to our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.

## Contact Us

For more information about our privacy practices, if you have questions, or if you would like to make a complaint, please contact us by e-mail at the address listed on the contact section of the site.
